Kids Exercise Their Minds - You Focus On YOU!

At Club Fitness, we understand how hard it can be to find time to work out, especially for parents. That’s why we’ve gone the extra mile to develop the very best Kids Club experience; to ensure your kids are well taken care of so you can exercise with peace of mind.

To ensure our Kids Club is industry-leading, Club Fitness has partnered with Brain Balance, which is designed to teach children about behavioral skills, focus, academic performance, impulsiveness, and much more. Fundamentally, the Brain Balance approach focuses on the concept of finding the balance between the left brain and the right brain. The left brain is the academic, cognitive side where motor skills, language, speech, math skills, and memory take place; while the right brain is the behavioral and emotional center where attention, impulse control, reading comprehension, and emotional reactions take place.
